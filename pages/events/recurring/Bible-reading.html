Fall 2023 & Spring 2024 - Join our weekly Bible readings!

We are meeting every Saturday from 7:30am to 9am to read the Bible starting from Genesis. We read the Bible for 30 minutes, share one thing we learned, and make breakfast together. This semester, one of our alumn who lives on Loyola Avenue (near Loyola Hall) is kind enough to host our Bible reading meetings on Saturdays :)

Contact us for his address. Also note that those joining the Bible reading and breakfast are bringing ingredients that we will cook together and share (eggs, fruits, etc.)
