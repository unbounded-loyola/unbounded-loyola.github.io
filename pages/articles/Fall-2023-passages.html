Bible passages for Fall 2023

WE ARE READING AND DISCUSSING 10 BIBLE PASSAGES THIS SEMESTER.

September 1, 2023 Unbounded Team

The student leaders are taking turn every week to lead each passage. This semester, our passages revolves around the theme of meeting Jesus and how it changes us. Whether it's John talking about Jesus as life and light or Luke showing healing and forgiveness, it's a journey of transformation. Taking up our cross and following Jesus challenges us to live out our faith. It's about encountering Christ and how that shapes us as college students, making us think about how faith brings real change to our lives.

Here are the passages we are discussing this semester:

  1. John 1:1-5 - “Who is Life & Light?”
  2. John 5:1-9 - “Get up! Pick up your mat and walk”
  3. Luke 19:1-10 - “Jesus Came to Seek and to Save the Lost.”
  4. Luke 15:11-24 - “The Parable of the Prodigal Son”
  5. Luke 23:13-49 - “Father, Forgive Them”
  6. Luke 24:1-48 - “He is Not Here; He Has Risen”
  7. Luke 5:1-11 - “You Will Fish for People”
  8. Mark 8:27-38 - “Take Up Your Cross and Follow Me”
  9. Thanksgiving Passage - Psalm 100:1-5 - “Give Thanks to the Lord”
  10. John 15:1-12 - “Remain in Jesus”
