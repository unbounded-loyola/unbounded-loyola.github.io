A fruitful year with Unbounded!

God has worked in our lives throughout Fall 2022 - Spring 2023 school year.

May 1, 2023 Unbounded Team

Praise God for an amazing school year! During Fall 2022 semester, we had 3 weekly Bible discussions on 9-step Bible study, a Bible study series to learn about the fundamentals of our faith, growing to know who God is, what He did, and what we do in response to His grace. Our first group met in person on Tuesdays every week where 3 students joined faithfully and took turn to lead every week. Our second group met on Thursday evenings, and their discussions were hybrid (in person at the IC and on Zoom); two students joined, and one recent grad participated as well. And our last group met on Fridays with one graduate student and 3 undergrads. All those who joined actively participated in the discussions and wrote testimonial reflections on the studied passages twice during the semester.

Then God blessed our Spring 2023 semester. We learned about the 7 "I am" statements of Jesus, teaching about the identity of God and His qualities. Students who have never led a meeting before took a leap of faith to lead at least one time this semester. What an amazing work of God! We are planning to have weekly Bible readings from Genesis to Revelation starting Fall 2023 to give students an opportunity to go through the Bible at least once before they graduate.
