Hear brief Testimonies from our students!

Our members shared brief testimonies on how the weekly discussions have helped them know more about Jesus.

May 1, 2023 Unbounded Team

Watch a short video on how Jesus has worked in the lives of our members through weekly Bible studies! As young Christians, getting to know Jesus throughout our college years is essential. We want to use this precious time in our lives to grow closer to our Lord. It's not an easy process, but we're here for each other :)

YouTube video: https://youtu.be/nixAfzI9bX4

"Carry each other’s burdens, and in this way, you will fulfill the law of Christ." Galatians 6:2

- Sam has grown closer to God through Bible studies. He likes the discussion environment and the dept of our Bible studies. He has witnessed the spiritual fruits of faithfully coming to God every week though God’s word.

- Victor likes our Bible centered group and has been growing spiritually through the teachings of Jesus. He is a master’s student in Software Engineering; although he is very busy with his degree, he decided to put Jesus at the center.

- Angie overcame her doubts and struggles because Jesus has been with her to carry her burdens. God’s word is strengthening her to go through tough college experiences. Coming to God every week has helped her feel His presence and have a meaningful connection to Him.

- Janice has learned a lot about God and feels much closer to Him through Bible discussions and testimony writings and sharing. She believes that God has a purpose for all of us and is looking forward to learning more about Him.

- Justin has been struggling to connect with God, but through Bible studies he gained deeper understanding of the Bible and who God is. He grew spiritually and his relationship to God now feels real and sincere.
